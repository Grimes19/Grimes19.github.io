GRiMES ARCH started about 80 years ago with the late Michael Lawerence Grimes the First. He used his skills of wood working to create beautiful furniture pieces for his neighborhood. Today, we have taken that passion as a family to a new level. Crafting not only modern homes with nature always in mind, but also custom furniture to fill that home. At GRIMES ARCH, we can help your visions of architecture come to life.

This chair was designed by my Grandpa all the way back in 1965. Before my father was even born. As you can see, his skills were unmatched for his time. His eye for detail is what we carry on in every work we touch. Below is the custom desk he crafted where so many of his masterpieces came to be. You can find that desk at our headquarters today in Jackson, Wyoming.
